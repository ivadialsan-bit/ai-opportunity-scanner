# PLAYBOOK

## Purpose

This document defines the first manual validation workflow for AI Opportunity Scanner.

## Package

Name: Digital request point in 48 hours.

Components:
- short company review;
- simple request page;
- contact buttons;
- FAQ;
- service text cleanup;
- call script;
- chat script;
- request tracking table.

## Price ladder

- pilot: 9 900 RUB;
- standard: 14 900 RUB;
- extended package: 29 000 RUB.

## Manual validation workflow

1. Select one niche and one city cluster.
2. Generate a CSV list of companies.
3. Sort companies by Lead Score.
4. Manually check top companies.
5. Prepare a short review for each promising company.
6. Ask permission to send the review.
7. Track response, interest, proposal and result.

## Metrics

For the first 100 targeted contacts:
- 5-10 permissions to send a short review is acceptable;
- 3-5 concrete proposals is acceptable;
- 0-1 payment is realistic at the beginning.

## Rules

Do not promise guaranteed revenue growth.
Do not build a large dashboard before validation.
Do not use automated bulk contact.
Do not sell AI as the value. Sell a concrete improvement package.
