# MASTER_CONTEXT

Project: AI Opportunity Scanner.

Purpose: find small service businesses with visible digital gaps and prepare a simple 48-hour improvement package.

First-week focus: collect companies, verify manually, prepare short reviews, make offers, track results.

Main package: Digital request point in 48 hours.

Initial niches: AC installation, appliance repair, cleaning, auto service, beauty.

Not in scope: SaaS, complex bots, long dashboard work, universal crawler.
